Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 8, 2025

1. Information We Collect

FocusCue is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Usage Data: Basic analytics about how you use our website (page views, time spent)
  • Local Storage: Your timer settings and scripts are stored locally in your browser only
  • Technical Data: Browser type, IP address, and device information for functionality
  • Premium Purchases: Payment information is processed by Gumroad (we don't store card details)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our service
  • Process premium purchases
  • Send important updates about the service
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data security is important to us:

  • Local Storage: Scripts and settings are stored in your browser's local storage
  • No Cloud Sync: We don't sync your personal data to our servers
  • HTTPS: All connections are encrypted using industry-standard SSL
  • UK/EU Servers: Our website is hosted on UK-based servers

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies for functionality:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics (with your consent)
  • No Third-Party Ads: We don't use advertising cookies
  • Your Choice: You can manage cookie preferences in the consent banner

5. Your Rights (GDPR/UK GDPR)

Under UK and EU data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Object: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@focus-cue.com

6. Third-Party Services

  • Gumroad: Processes premium payments (has its own privacy policy)
  • Cloudflare: Provides security and performance (minimal data processing)
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics (optional, with consent)

7. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored until you clear your browser data
  • Analytics: Anonymized data retained for 24 months maximum
  • Support Emails: Deleted after 12 months
  • Purchase Records: Retained for 7 years for tax purposes

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our website. Continued use of FocusCue after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data:

  • Email: support@focus-cue.com
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 48 hours
  • Data Protection Authority: You can also contact the ICO (UK) or your local DPA
