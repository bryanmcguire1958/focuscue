Frequently Asked Questions

What features does FocusCue include?

FocusCue is completely free and includes professional countdown timers (up to 5 minutes), teleprompter with auto-scroll and step modes, adjustable focus dot, OBS browser source integration, keyboard shortcuts, and auto-saving settings. Perfect for content creators and presenters.

Is FocusCue really free?

Yes! FocusCue is completely free to use. No subscriptions, no hidden fees, no sign-up required. Just open your browser and start presenting professionally.

Does it work with OBS Studio?

Absolutely! FocusCue works perfectly with OBS Studio. Simply add it as a browser source using the provided URL and enable transparent background.

What operating systems are supported?

FocusCue is web-based and works on any OS with a modern browser - Windows, Mac, Linux, ChromeOS, and even mobile devices.

Do I need to create an account?

No account needed! FocusCue works directly in your browser. Your settings and teleprompter scripts are saved locally on your device for privacy and convenience.
